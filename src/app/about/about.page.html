About MoMoCharges

MoMoCharges is a 100% free mobile money charges calculator. It helps you know your transaction charges for Orange, MTN, and Express Union.

 

With it's transaction charge comparison feature, you can compare the cost of sending money through any of the mobile money operators.

 

It's a Progressive Web Application (PWA) that has full offline capability, meaning you can add it to your Home Screen and use like any other app.

 

Feel free to get in touch for any issues and/or suggestions.
